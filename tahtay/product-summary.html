III. Branding: Logo Label Analysis (Durability & Quality)

The label selection prioritizes **highest graphic quality, vibrant colors, and non-fading wash durability** (hundreds of washes).

Label Style Pros (High Quality & Color) Cons (Durability & Fading)
**Woven Label** Excellent durability and high wash resilience. Premium, classic feel. Colors are thread-limited; fine graphic details and gradients are difficult to reproduce. Graphic quality is good, not photographic.
**Laser-Cut Patch (Leather/Faux)** Highly durable; absolutely no fading. Provides a high-end, tactile, and rugged aesthetic. No vibrant colors—design is typically monochromatic (etched/burnt). Graphic detail is limited to the etching process.
**Printed Label (High-Density)** Highest graphic detail (photographic quality possible) and most vibrant color reproduction (full spectrum). Least wash-durable; colors may crack, peel, or fade significantly over repeated, heavy washing, failing the 'hundreds of washes' criteria.

**Conclusion for Branding:** The High-Definition Woven Label offers the best balance, providing high wash resilience essential for a functional product while delivering good visual quality.
