Art Prints for Sale

Print information

In addition to unique physical media, many of our artists' works are available as art prints.


We offer art glicée prints of featured works, on either stretched canvas or matted and framed high-quality photo paper. Framing services are provided by The Paint Chip in Davis. Dye-sublimation prints on other media can be made via special order.


All prints are made in extremely limited editions, and are hand-signed and numbered by the original artist, using archival stable inks.
